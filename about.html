ABOUT ME

Reina Mmesoma Ezeabasilim is a thriving software developer, passionate about creating highly-functional, user-centred softwares currently a Fellow at TIIDELAB.

She is skilled in HTML, CSS, Javascript as well as User Experience/User Interface skills; Figma and Adobe XD.

She has experience designing effective user experience and has collaborated on some projects including a baby care app and a Medical assistance Dementia app.

Reina holds a degree in Chemical Engineering from Nnamdi Azikiwe university Awka and is a member of the Nigerian Society of Chemical Engineers.

She enjoys taking on challenges, learning new technologies, problem-solving and networking.
